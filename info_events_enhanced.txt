v1.4 (in development)
	Changed Events (Gameplay):
		Aeron's chosen

			- Now takes feature health into account (MagisterCultuum)
		Flare Nature Node
			- Now takes elven forest on imrpovements into account (MagisterCultuum)
		Dedicate Monument
			- Sucellus now gives +3 Health instead of +1 (Balance against +2 Food from Amathaon) (MagisterCultuum)
		Penguins
			- Triggers on flat snow only, instead of Snow or Tundra (MagisterCultuum)
		Sailor's Dirge
			- Now 60% of games active, instead of 20% (MagisterCultuum)
		Sickness
			- Added option for Infernals: Receive Champion (No sickness for Infernals) (MagisterCultuum)
		Sign of Arawn
			- Immobilizes all unded units for 1 turn (MagisterCultuum/Changed)
			- Added option for Sidar: Golden Age (MagisterCultuum)
		Sign of Aeron
			- Added option for Calabim: Golden Age (MagisterCultuum)
		Sign of Bhall
			- Added option for Clan: Golden Age (MagisterCultuum)
		Sign of Mammon
			- Now gives 100 Gold instead of 40 (MagisterCultuum/Changed)
		Sign of Mulcarn
			- Now gives +1 Production from Temple of the Hand (MagisterCultuum)
		Traitor
			- Calabim feast option gives 50 Culture and -10 Crime (MagisterCultuum)
		Undercouncil
			- Option 2 gives all recon units City Raider 1 instead of Magic Resistance
		Witch
			- Executing Witch now adds +2 to AC
		
		Demon Sign
			- 3rd option gives priest instead of high priest (was OP, I don't want national units to be given)
			- Grigori no longer receive a luonnotar for 50G and 1 temp unhappy (see above), instead they can pay 10G to let nothing happen.
		Hanging Sisters
			- 1st option now properly only for evil
		Pacifist demonstration
			- 4th option gives the best available non-national-unit warrior upgrade instead of an Axeman
			- 5th option gives 3 XP to all vampires instead of only to the ones in the city, removed +food in city (you don't get it from feast either)
		Strange Adept
			- Recruiting the Adept now adds +1 to AC (Make it more a "sin" for Good Civs)
			- Expelling the Adept now gives -1 to AC instead of +1 temporary happiness
		Scholars
			- Now requires Writing AND Knowledge of the Ether (Just being able to click "We cannot afford it", because only having Writing and no library is not fun)
			- Prices halved (was far too much)
		That Kind of Day
			- Does not trigger for players with peace with the barbs
			- Now searches plots around the city properly
		Trapped Frostlings
			- Releasing Frostlings now gives -1 to AC instead of +1 Happy for 15 turns (mercy)

		Removed huns, vandals, goths, philistines, vedic aryans python callbacks
